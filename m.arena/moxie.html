Design Mania

Design is a communication- intensive activity in which both words and pictures are used, and written and oral forms are employed. If the point of contact between the product and people becomes a point of friction, then the designer has failed. On the other hand, if people are made safer, more efficient, more comfortable- or just plain happier- by contact with the product, then the designer has succeeded.
