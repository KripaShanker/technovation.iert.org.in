Technovation

Technovation is the Annual Technical Fest of IERT, Allahabad. Organised jointly by the third year. There are more than 50+ events. Some of the events are technical and some are non-technical. Some events specialise to certain stream, but everyone try to participate in maximum events. Some of the events are huge that's why we calll it Mega evvent of Techovation, Like Kavyanjali, War of Bands, Codervation. The events see the huge participation from Engineering Degree Division along with Diploma. Other college of Allahabad also came to participate and give neck to neck competition. Technovation is all about three days which are life time memories along with acivements.
